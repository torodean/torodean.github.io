About d0sag3 Films

D0sag3 Films (D3F) is a graphic design and video editing title that I created for myself in high school. D0sag3 (which is the word dosage spelled with a zero and a three) was my Xbox live gamertag when I was younger. After spending some time playing games such as Halo, I became interested in video editing and graphic design. This lead me to creating d0sag3 Films and beginning my graphic design career. Through the years, I have kept the d0sag3 Films name and used it whenever creating graphic art or video editing. As my professional career develops and I tend further in time away from being associated with my X-box live gamertag, consideration for a graphic design name change has been considered and will likely come in the near future.

d0sag3 Films (2012)

Many of my projects include introduction animation for gaming groups, banners and wallpapers. Some of my first projects included paid projects for Detroit In Focus where I created some of the introduction and transition scenes of a MMA promotional video found here. Solar Systems and Celestial Bodies (2018)

One thing that I've worked with is the creation of textures and animations for celestial bodies. This is done by creating planet textures that can be mapped to a sphere and then moving fractals for simulating star surfaces or atmospheric activities (clouds, lava flow, storms, etc).
